Sweet Potato Red Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 4 medium onions, diced
  • 4 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 and 1/2 cups dried red lentils, such as Gefen
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • Pereg Pepper, to taste
  • water, to cover

Directions:

  1. Heat oil in a large stock pot.
  2. Fry onions until translucent and soft.
  3. Add sweet potatoes. Cover with water and bring to a boil.
  4. Add lentils and spices and bring to a boil again.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for one and a half hours.
  6. Blend with immersion blender.

Tip: The best pots for soup are ones that are tall rather than wide. They save you space on your stovetop.

Blended Mushroom-Wine Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Tuscanini Olive Oil
  • 1 large Spanish onion, diced
  • 4 cubes Gefen Frozen Garlic
  • 4 portobello mushrooms
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms
  • 2 ounces cremini mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup fresh checked parsley
  • 1/4 cup sherry cooking wine
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Pereg Pepper
  • 4 and 1/2 cups water

Directions:

  1. In a medium-sized pot, heat oil. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant, about three minutes.
  2. Remove stems and gills from portobello and cremini mushrooms and slice. Slice button mushrooms. Add all mushrooms to the pot and stir. Sauté over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for 45 minutes.
  4. Blend with an immersion blender.

Creamiest Zucchiniest Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 3 zucchini, chunked
  • 4 cubes Gefen Frozen Garlic
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 4 ounces canned mushrooms
  • 1 potato, peeled and chunked
  • 1 teaspoon flour
  • 1 teaspoon Gefen Dill Weed
  • salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon parve chicken soup mix
  • 1/3 cup Gefen Almond Milk
  • 5 cups water

Directions:

  1. In a pot over low-medium heat, sauté vegetables for 15 minutes.
  2. Slowly stir in flour, then add dill, salt and pepper, chicken soup mix, almond milk, and water.
  3. Cover pot and bring to a boil.
  4. Lower flame and simmer for one hour.
  5. Blend with an immersion blender until desired consistency is reached.
