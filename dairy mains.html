Eggplant Lasag-mesan

Ingredients

  • 1 jar Tuscanini Marinara Sauce
  • 1 box Tuscanini Lasagna Noodles or other oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 1 bag frozen breaded eggplant, such as Tuscanini
  • 8-ounce bag shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup water

Directions:

  1. In a 9x13-inch pan, pour a thin layer of marinara sauce. Layer lasagna noodles, eggplant slices, sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat.
  2. Top with lasagna noodles, the rest of the sauce, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Pour the water around the sides of the pan. Cover and bake on 350 degrees Fahrenheit for one hour. For a crispy top, uncover and broil for five minutes.

Cheesy Chimichanga

Ingredients

  • 15 (6-in./15-cm.) wraps
  • 2 tablespoons oil, such as Gefen Canola Oil, plus more for frying
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 pound (450 grams) mushrooms, sliced (either fresh or canned)
  • 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Pereg Garlic Powder
  • pinch of black pepper
  • 8 ounces (225 grams) ricotta cheese
  • 8 ounces (225 grams) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups Gefen Cornflake Crumbs

Directions:

  1. Heat two tablespoons oil in a frying pan and sauté onion until it starts to turn golden. Add mushrooms and spinach and sauté another five to seven minutes. Add seasonings and remove from heat.
  2. Once vegetables have cooled, add in the cheeses and stir.
  3. Lay a wrap on a plate. Spoon two tablespoons of the veggie-cheese mixture in the center of the wrap. Fold in the two sides and then roll up. Lay on a tray seam-side down so they stay shut.
  4. When all the wraps have been filled, dip them one at a time in the beaten eggs and then roll in cornflake crumbs.
  5. Heat oil in a frying pan and fry wraps on each side until golden brown. Serve warm.

Note: I tried these in my Betty Crocker, and they came out perfect that way too!

Tip: Serve with some warmed marinara sauce for dipping.

Chimichangas are a deep-fried burrito that are common in Tex-Mex cuisine. I added the cornflake crumb layer for some added crunch.

Creamy Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 (16-ounce) box Tuscanini Spaghetti, cooked according to package directions
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 6 cubes Gefen Frozen Crushed Garlic
  • 8 ounces whipped cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions:

  1. Sauté garlic in oil just until fragrant.
  2. Add cream cheese, Parmesan cheese and milk and stir until smooth. Pour over spaghetti and mix.

Tip: Pasta and sauce can be prepped in advance (stored separately). Add a little milk to thin sauce when reheating.
