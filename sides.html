Marinated Grilled Vegetables

Ingredients

Grilled Vegetables

  • 2 small yellow squash
  • 1 eggplant
  • 2 zucchini
  • 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 2 Spanish onions, sliced

Marinade

  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Gefen Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Gefen Oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:

  1. Mix all ingredients for marinade.
  2. Wash squash and eggplant well. Cut into one-inch slices. Combine all vegetables.
  3. Pour marinade over vegetables. Marinate for two to four hours (or overnight).
  4. Place marinated vegetables onto baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes. Can be served hot or cold.

Big-Batch Shlishkes

Ingredients

  • 10 medium challos, soaked, squeezed and crust removed
  • 10 potatoes, unpeeled
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 10 cups Mishpacha Flour
  • 6 cups Pereg Bread Crumbs
  • 1 and 1/2 cups oil, such as Gefen Canola Oil

Directions:

  1. Cook potatoes in peels, in salted water. Drain, peel, and mash. Cool to room temperature.
  2. In a large bowl or mixer, combine potatoes, challah and flour, and knead to form a smooth dough.
  3. Fill a 16-quart pot with water and bring to a boil. Prepare two large roaster pans.
  4. In a separate frying pan, toast bread crumbs in oil until browned. Divide among the prepared roasters.
  5. On a floured board, roll out dough into long ropes a quarter inch wide. Cut dough into one-inch pieces and drop into boiling water. Boil for 10 minutes until shlishkes float to the top. Continue to cook for five minutes longer. Drain and toss into toasted bread crumbs. Reheat in tightly covered pans.

Note: Shlishkes can be prepared in advance. Freeze in a large roasted and reheat, covered, in oven at 350 for one hour, placing water in another large roaster on lower rack of oven. Freezes very well.

Onion-Noodle Kugelettes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 2 Spanish onions, diced
  • 1/2 pound Tuscanini Spaghetti, cooked and drained
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon Lipton Onion Soup Mix
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Pereg Pepper

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil. Lower flame to medium-low and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine spaghetti with remaining ingredients. Stir in half of the onions.
  3. Grease 12 standard-sized muffin tins. Pour spaghetti mixture into tins.
  4. Mix remaining egg mixture with remaining onions and pour on top.
  5. Bake covered at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes, then uncovered for five minutes.
